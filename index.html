About

I began my mandala journey in 2017, shortly before retiring from 30+ years in Corporate America, when a simple Pinterest photo sparked what has become a fulfilling passion. Drawn to symmetry and order, I find peace in the meditative process of building each design dot by dot, often guided by color, mood, or the view from my Mt. Hope window.

What started as therapy has grown into an art practice that grounds me in the moment and celebrates the beauty of balance. Mandalas represent wholeness, unity, and harmony, reminding me to slow down and stay present.
